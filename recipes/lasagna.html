Lasagna

Homemade

This is one of the easiest recipes you will find.

I promise you will not be disappointed.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes (400g can)
  • Cheese (400g)
  • Minced beef (500g)
  • One whole onion
  • Pasta sheets (5)

Steps

  1. Open can of tomatoes
  2. Brown mince beef
  3. Add chopped onion and tomatoes to beef
  4. Add cheese
